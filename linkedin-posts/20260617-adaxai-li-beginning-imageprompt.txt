High-contrast linework, dense crosshatching for shadow and texture, pure ink on aged parchment — no color, no grayscale gradients. Hand-drawn imperfection, not vector-clean. FIRST IMAGE PROMPT ("the beginning of the journey")
=====================================================

Black-and-white pen-and-ink woodcut illustration, fantasy RPG chronicle tome style, heavy crosshatching, aged parchment texture, decorative border. A hooded scribe sits at a wooden desk, quill in hand, writing in a massive leather-bound book. Beside the book sits a small spectral familiar made of glowing circuit-like lines (rendered in the same ink style, no color) — a magical creature representing an AI companion. Through a window behind the desk, a flying cauldron drifts among the clouds. Candlelight, dramatic ink shadows, no color, square composition.
